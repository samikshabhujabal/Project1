Accessibility Notes

  • Contrast Ratios: All text colors meet WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text)
  • Color Blindness: Red/Blue combination works for most types of color blindness
  • Focus States: All interactive elements have visible focus indicators
  • High Contrast Mode: System respects user's high contrast preferences
  • Semantic Colors: Colors have consistent meaning across the interface
